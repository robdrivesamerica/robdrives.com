FAQ & Policies

Do you publish prices?

Service is bespoke and by appointment. Most bookings are hourly with a 2-hour minimum (3-hour on weekends). Receive a tailored quote after you share details.

Whose vehicle is used?

Yours. For protection, clients typically add Rob as a permitted or named driver on their policy for the period of service.

24h full refund; within 24h = 50%; no-show = 100%. 15-minute grace period for wait time, then billed in 30-minute increments.
